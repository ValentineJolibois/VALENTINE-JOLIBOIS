Valentine Jolibois creates installations where soft and hard materials contrast, blending seductive beauty with serious themes. Inspired by the shell, both armor and prison, she explores protection, vulnerability, and ecological issues. Her works transform objects and supports into interactive and poetic sculptures.

Trained in sculpture at ENSAV La Cambre, she also studied at the Gerrit Rietveld Academie and the Lyon School of Fine Arts. In 2024, she was awarded the Prix des Amis de La Cambre, granted to ten graduates in recognition of the quality of their diploma projects. Valentine lives in Brussels and co-founded the collective Elvy Tremor with Léonore Bienert.
